About

This app works out your overtime and subsistence for each shift, then keeps a running record across the week and month. It is built for paramedics and other shift workers who claim overtime and away-from-base allowances and still fill in a paper timesheet.

Scan your incident screen or enter calls by hand, set your roster once, and the app does the calculation. You check the figures and write them onto your sheet. The app assists, you assert, nothing is submitted anywhere on your behalf.

Your data stays on this device. Photos are read on the device and never uploaded. You can back up or clear your data at any time, it is yours.
